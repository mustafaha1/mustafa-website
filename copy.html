Unit 2, Outcome 1 1.5 Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites

Copyright is essentially a private right. You, the copyright owner must decide how to exploit your copyright work and how to enforce your copyright. A copyright owner can decide whether there will be any use of the copyright work falling within the scope of the economic rights and, if so, whether he or she will use the copyright work and/or license one or more other people to use the work. Copyright and other intellectual property laws affect websites as it may restrict what you are publish publishing.
